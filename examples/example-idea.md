# The 5 minutes before your alarm

Layer 1 / Awareness A. Targets the universal "morning routine" discussion but shifts focus to the narrow window BEFORE the routine starts — the 5 minutes where you're still deciding whether to get up. Pattern interrupt: everyone talks about routines, nobody talks about the decision window.

No product mention. No CTA. Just the reframe.

## Pseudo Script

everyone talks about their morning routine

what they eat

what they drink

when they meditate

nobody talks about the 5 minutes before the alarm

the 5 minutes where you decide if you even get up

that's the routine

the rest is execution
